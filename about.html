Erza is a young woman with long, scarlet hair and brown eyes. She lost her right eye as a child[9] and now has an artificial one which was created by Porlyusica.[10] She has a slender, voluptuous figure that Lucy Heartfilia described as "amazing". Her Fairy Tail stamp is blue and is located on the middle of her left upper arm.
